Mission Statement

Our mission is to help pave the way for economic prosperity for local businesses and the communities they serve during times of adversity. 

How we can help

Below are brief descriptions ​of a few of the ways our team can help you get your business back on track.

Consulting

Our team's expertise in management, IT, finance, and operations can help your business come out of COVID-19 with stronger foundations than ever before. 

Financial Aid

Sweat, no equity

Every business is different. We will work with a selected applicant to help improve their outlook in any way within our means.

Application

If you are interested in our services, please click on the button below and fill out the form it redirects you to. Our team will thoroughly review your application, reach out to you with further questions if needed, and notify you when we come to a decision.
We're primarily looking for applicants who meet the following general requirements. 
  • Located in the DFW metroplex
  • Legal entity prior to January 2020/the onset of COVID-19
  • Owner/founder applies or is in agreement with receiving our services
  • Business income and workforce impacted significantly due to the COVID-19 pandemic
